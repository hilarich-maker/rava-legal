Child Safety & CSAE Policy

Last updated: January 2026

RAVA is a women-only community platform designed exclusively for adults. We are committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and supportive environment for all users.

Zero Tolerance Policy

RAVA has a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Any content, behavior, communication, or activity involving minors in a sexual context is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate and permanent account removal.

Age Restriction

RAVA is intended only for users aged 18 and above.

Accounts suspected of belonging to minors, or accounts that attempt to misrepresent age, will be suspended and permanently removed from the platform.

Monitoring & Enforcement

RAVA actively moderates the platform and reviews all reports related to safety violations.

CSAE-related content or behavior will result in:

  • Immediate content removal
  • Permanent account termination
  • Reporting to relevant authorities when required by law

Reporting & Contact

Users may report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app or by contacting us at:

Email: support@rava-community.com

All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with seriousness and discretion.

Commitment to Safety

RAVA is committed to maintaining a respectful and safe community environment. The platform has community guidelines and moderation practices designed to reduce the risk of harm, exploitation, or abuse.
