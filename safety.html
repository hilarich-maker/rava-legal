# Child Safety & CSAE Policy _Last updated: January 2026_ RAVA is a women-only community platform designed exclusively for adults. --- ## Zero Tolerance Policy RAVA has a **zero-tolerance policy** toward any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content, behavior, communication, or activity involving minors in a sexual context is **strictly prohibited** and will result in immediate and permanent account removal. --- ## Age Restriction RAVA is intended **only for users aged 18 and above**. Accounts that are suspected to belong to minors, or that attempt to misrepresent age, will be suspended and permanently removed from the platform. --- ## Monitoring & Enforcement RAVA actively moderates the platform and reviews user reports related to safety violations. Any CSAE-related content or behavior will be: - Immediately removed - Result in permanent account termination - Reported to relevant authorities when required by law --- ## Reporting & Contact Users can report inappropriate content or behavior directly within the app or by contacting us at: **Email:** support@rava-community.com All reports are reviewed promptly and handled with seriousness and discretion. --- ## Commitment to Safety RAVA is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and supportive environment for all users. We continuously work to uphold community standards and protect our users from harmful content and behavior.
