RAVA Single Women Community App – Privacy Policy RAVA respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how RAVA collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in connection with the use of the RAVA mobile application. 1. Information We Collect We may collect the following: Name Email City Profile photo Verification link or social link Chat messages Reports, blocks, and interactions in the app Device and usage data Information may be provided directly by users or collected automatically through the use of the App. 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: Operate and maintain the app Verify user profiles Prevent fraud and impersonation Manage reports and community safety Improve user experience Offer premium features We do not sell your information to third parties. We process personal data based on user consent, the performance of the App’s services, compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate interests related to community safety and platform integrity. 3. Sharing Information We may share information only when required by law or to protect users from harm. We do not share chat content externally unless required for safety or legal purposes. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who assist in operating the App (such as hosting, infrastructure, or communication services), solely for the purpose of providing the App’s functionality. 4. Storage and Security We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no digital system is fully secure. Personal information may be stored and processed on servers located in different jurisdictions, subject to applicable data protection laws. Use of the app is at your own risk. 5. User Controls You may: Edit your profile Delete your account Block or report other users Users may request access to their personal data by contacting support or request deletion of their personal data by clicking on the delete account button on the profile page. We may retain certain information where required by law or for safety, security, or dispute resolution purposes. Deleting your account removes your profile, but some anonymized data may remain for safety or legal reasons. 6. Data Retention We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to operate the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data and the purpose of processing. 7. Children RAVA is not intended for users under 18. 8. Updates to Policy We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app means acceptance of the updated policy. Material changes to this Privacy Policy may be communicated through the App or by other reasonable means. 9. Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you may contact us at: support@rava-community.com
